# Lecture 1. Introduction to Open Source


## **Summary**

In this lecture, we will explore the basic concepts of open source. You will be guided to understand the freedoms related to open source, what is open source is, and what it is not.
We will also navigate the history of open source, going through the important milestones that the movement has gone through until the present.
