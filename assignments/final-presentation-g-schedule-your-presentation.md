### What should we present?

The presentation is aimed at walking me through your contribution process and your contribution itself.

Points that may be considered:

- What did you learn about the project
- Issue choice: how was it?
- Documentation:
  - The good, the bad, the ugly
- Contribution process:
  - How was it?
  - Problems? (and solutions)
  - What did you learn?
- Your contribution
  - How was it?
  - What did you learn?
  - Please show me what you've done and your reasoning process
  - Submitted? Accepted?

I would spend a bit more than half of the time on the contribution process and the contribution.

You may use slides, demos, show documents, and show code. It can be the way you prefer! I want to know how you did it, what you did, what you've learned.
