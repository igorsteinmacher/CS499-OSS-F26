### Assignment: Proposing a New Governance Model for OpenMyProject

**(References and materials are provided at the end)**

#### **Background:**

OpenMyProject is a mature open-source project currently governed by a benevolent dictator model with a few core members. To promote inclusivity, transparency, and a stronger sense of community, the project is seeking a new governance model. Your task is to propose a new governance model that involves the community in the decision-making process.

#### **Objectives:**

1. Understand different governance models for open-source projects.
2. Propose a governance model that increases inclusivity and transparency.
3. Define a set of rules and guidelines for the new governance model.
4. Ensure the community is involved in the proposal process.

#### **Assignment Instructions:**

1. **Research and Analysis:**

   - Research different open-source governance models (e.g., meritocratic, consensus-based, federated, etc.).
   - Analyze the strengths and weaknesses of these models, especially in the context of inclusivity and transparency.
2. **Proposal Development:**

   - Develop a proposal for a new governance model for OpenMyProject. Your proposal should address the following:
     - **Structure:** Describe the structure of the proposed governance model. Include roles, responsibilities, and decision-making processes.
     - **Inclusivity:** Explain how the new model promotes inclusivity within the community.
     - **Transparency:** Outline mechanisms for ensuring transparency in decision-making.
     - **Community Involvement:** Propose ways to involve the community in the governance process (e.g., regular meetings, voting systems, open forums).
3. **Rule Definition:**

   - Define a set of rules and guidelines for the new governance model. Consider aspects such as:
     - Code of conduct
     - Contribution guidelines
     - Conflict resolution mechanisms
     - Voting and decision-making procedures
4. **Community Engagement Plan:**

   - Develop a plan for engaging the community in the transition to the new governance model. This should include:
     - Communication strategies (e.g., newsletters, social media updates)
     - Feedback mechanisms (e.g., surveys, public discussions)
     - Implementation timeline and milestones
5. **Presentation:**

   - Prepare a presentation summarizing your proposal. Your presentation should be clear, concise, and persuasive.
   - Include visual aids (e.g., diagrams, charts) to illustrate the structure and processes of the proposed governance model.

#### Submission Requirements:

1. **Written Proposal:** A detailed document (4-6 pages) outlining your proposed governance model, including the research analysis, structure, rules, and community engagement plan.
2. **Presentation:** A 5-7-minute recorded presentation using a slide deck (~10 slides) summarizing your proposal.
3. **Engagement Plan:** A separate document (2 pages) detailing your community engagement plan, including communication strategies and implementation timeline.

#### Evaluation Criteria:

- **Research and Analysis:** Depth and relevance of research on governance models.
- **Proposal Quality:** Clarity, feasibility, and comprehensiveness of the proposed governance model.
- **Inclusivity and Transparency:** Effectiveness of measures to promote inclusivity and transparency.
- **Community Engagement:** Quality and practicality of the community engagement plan.
- **Presentation:** Clarity, organization, and persuasiveness of the presentation.
